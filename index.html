Chocolate Ice Cream

This is a good recipe for Ice Cream, in this particular case, Chocolate.

Ingredients
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Milk
  • Sugar

YouÂ´ll also need a scale, to measure everything correctly.

A good manual beater will be enough for this.

An empty jar to pour the mix.

Quantities
  • 200g. Cocoa
  • 300g. Milk
  • 400g. Sugar

2ml. vanilla essence, distilled version, could be "Flavor"

2ml. mantecado essence, distilled version, could be "Flavor"

2L. of water, that will be poured in a pot

1st Step

Measure everything, heat the water, add gradually sugar, milk, cocoa, essences and stir, until it boils.

2nd step

After cooling the mix, blend it with CMC, not with a manual mixer, but with a blender, until it gets creamy.

After that, put them in the freezer, wait until it getsto the desired consistency and enjoy.
